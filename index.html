Reservation Management: Hotel staff can manage reservations efficiently, including creating, modifying, and canceling bookings. Automated notifications are sent to guests to confirm their reservations and provide relevant details. Staff can efficiently manage guest arrivals and departures, update room status, and handle payment transactions seamlessly. Staff receive notifications promptly, enabling timely responses to guest requests and ensuring a pleasant stay experience.
